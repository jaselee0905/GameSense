Hello, we are Game Sense

In the beginning... Gamesense – A North American professional eSports organization that strives off community, gaming, and dominating the playing field. We aim to reach greatness and refuse to accept defeat. No matter win or lose, each moment is an opportunity to learn and adapt. Our organization promises to uphold integrity by showing good sportsmanship and commitment inside and outside the game.
Our Management – Gamesense was founded and created by Tien Nguyen and Noah Lyles. Starting Gamesense in December of 2021, our experienced team has opened up great opportunities for us to pursue, with no time wasted, we believe every goal is obtainable.
Our Goal – Starting as a CS:GO ESEA Advanced team we hope to bring in more skilled individuals to help us compete in other eSport titles. Not only will we strive for perfection in eSports, but we will build a unique brand, getting us seen all around the world.
Our Reasons – We've grown tired of seeing false promises, failed teams, and a lack of competition. Gamesense wants to change that. Our Future – To build a large tight-knit community with a positive gaming culture and lifestyle. Showcasing the up and coming talent around the world and giving them the proper support they need to succeed!

Join our community on discord, there's always giveaways, and events going on every week.
